The African Institute for Health Policy

At the Africa Institute for Health Policy Foundation (AIHP) we recognize that health organizations are operating in an increasingly complex environment characterized by uncertainty and constant evolution. The business as usual approach is no longer tenable, AIHP works with other stakeholders in the Africa’s health landscape to anticipate these changes and adapt accordingly through the adoption of targeted innovations. The AIHP experts have an in-depth understanding of the Africa’s health landscape and an adaptable outlook that allows for meaningful implementation even in the most complex environments.

We partner with others to create scalable solutions for the pressing population health challenges facing the African continent. AIHP flagship interventions embrace a holistic approach to the health system, covering all its components ranging from closing the gaps in service delivery and human resources to bridging the health technology and information divide.

Some of the thematic areas that are covered by the AIHP and its partners include:



  • Health Policy Research
  • Data analytics and decisions support
  • Advisory services
  • Incubation of Novel Health Solutions
  • Educational and Training Services
  • Strategic Information Solutions


Our Service Portfolio

Health Policy Research

Our goal is to advance health decision making processes towards a firmer evidential base purposely to promote health system performance and accountability. AIHP and partners conduct sustained and timely research and analysis on the major elements of health policy in Africa as a key ingredient for effective decision making and implementation. Engaging policy makers and practitioners in substantially shaping discourse to fill a critical need in forward looking pragmatic policy analysis defines our primary objective.


Data analytics and decisions support

At AIHP we are confident that careful examination of data can yield useful insights to catalyze innovations and drive improvements in the healthcare ecosystem. Measurement and performance assessment is management tool that would enable health stewards make informed strategic decisions. Be it conducting cutting edge research aimed at assisting our clients to gain in-depth understanding of the prevailing population health needs; or undertaking rigorous quantitative analysis, AIHP is able to deliver. Our expertise in health data analytics are brought to bear in assisting our prized clients. We embrace an innovative analytical framework that triangulates data from different sources to provide critical information for decision making. We conduct practical assessments for socially minded customers wishing to make a sustainable impact.


Advisory services

HOur advisory services are underpinned on a strong research and evidential base that employs the most current and best of class analytical methods in health metrics and evaluation while maintaining the practical and adaptable flair suitable for a dynamic business ecosystem in Africa. Through our multidisciplinary skill set, AIHP has proven capabilities in different facets of the healthcare value chain in the African region.
Ranging from optimization of clinical research and service delivery to generation of nuanced insights through market research, we are able to assist our clients realize their broad organizational objectives in diverse settings and areas of operation. Overall, AIHP is at the forefront in all aspects of evidence management in the healthcare space that creates value for our clients.


Incubation of Novel Health Solutions

AIHP collaborates with entrepreneurs, practitioners, policy-makers, academics and organizations of various types to catalyze innovations, build health system capacity and scale up effective health interventions. In this quest our efforts are ambitious but our resources limited. Therefore, we believe that sustainable impactful change should and will happen through collaboration. By bringing together various stakeholders within a community building framework; expertise and talent will drive creative interventions and solutions. The first Summit of this type was held in Mozambique on the 10th October 2013 in partnership with the University St. Thomas of Mozambique.


Educational and Training Services

Quality of care is at the core of our service delivery model. We are cognizant of the fact that provision of quality health services is impossible without a pool of well trained personnel. Therefore, to ensure that we provide quality and effective health services in a sustainability manner, training and mentoring form an integral part of programming in all our projects. At AIHP we leverage our comparative advantages and of our partners to offer practical training in critical areas of the health system. We cover areas as disparate as executive training for health system managers and on-hand clinical skills development for frontline health workers at different levels. The Educational and Training component is strongly linked to our health service delivery initiatives at different levels of care.


Strategic Information Solutions

AIHP provides strategic monitoring, evaluation and research capabilities vital for successful implementation of health programs. Through a pool of experienced experts who have provided such support in different countries within the continent, AIHP endeavors to promote monitoring and evaluation as a tool for effective health system management. Through our educational and training activities we further strive to nurture future leaders in the field of monitoring and evaluation as means towards stronger health systems.





Our Team

The AIHP boasts a deep bench of experienced team members with proven competence and expertise in different disciplines of healthcare, including data and business analytics. Most team members also have vast experience in the African continent, with most of them having extensively worked in different international settings.
Our team has a passion in developing innovative solutions for clients that are looking to have a social and sustainable impact in the continent. The focus is all encompassing and aimed at achievement of sustainable development. We never lose sight of the clients’ needs while promoting the community developmental needs.



TOM ACHOKI

Founder & CEO

Tom Achoki, is a graduate of the prestigious MIT Sloan School of Management where he was a Sloan Fellow and earned an MBA degree. Tom has been a Director of African Initiatives and Clinical Assistant Professor at the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation (IHME) at the University of Washington. He has over twelve years’ progressive experience in population health research, program management and technical advisory roles at country, regional, and global levels. Tom is an adjunct faculty member at the School of Health Systems and Public Health at the University of Pretoria, South Africa.

He has served in technical and leadership positions in both the public and private sector organizations where he has successfully delivered complex population health projects. His career trajectory has seen him live and work in various countries, including Kenya, South Sudan, South Africa, Democratic Republic of Congo, Switzerland, Botswana and currently in the United States.

Dr. Achoki, is a medical doctor and has specialized in public health and tropical medicine from the University of Pretoria in South Africa. He has also completed a PhD from the Utrecht University in the Netherlands and a two-year Post-Graduate Fellowship at the University of Washington’s Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation.
